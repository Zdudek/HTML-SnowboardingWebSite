Mountains of choice!

Excellent beginner mountains in NH:
McINTRYRE Ski Area

       Awesome ski area for the family! It's fairly cheap and its in Manchester so, its nice and close by. It's not much of a mountain, as far as mountains go, more like a large hill. This fact makes it an awesome beginner mountain! Definitly worth checking it out if you haven't been.


PatsPeak

       A great mountain located in Henniker, NH. It is about a 40 minute drive give or take. The best part about pats peak is that it offers reasonably priced lift tickets and if you are a student or a military personnel you recieve a discount. It's very family friendly and offers a wonder progressional bunny slope set up. Check out the Link to see the trail map and learn more about the mountain.

Season Passes for College students in NH and NE(specificly Maine)

Huge savings if you plan on multiple trips of snowboarding in a year!

New England College Pass

        The 2013-14 New England College Pass gives you direct-to-lift access to ski and ride Loon Mountain, Sunday River and Sugarloaf all on one season pass - with no blackout dates! That's access to a total of 351 trails, 1,944 acres, 17 terrain parks and 6 halfpipes - all for only $349!,if you purchase early. Valid every day of the 2013-14 season. No restrictions, no blackout days. Available to college students carrying 9 credits or more at time of pass pick-up in fall. Valid college ID and letter from registrar's office required at pass pick up.

4NH College Pass

        The 4-NH College Pass offers unlimited skiing and riding on 255 trails, 37 lifts, 1,061 acres and 13 terrain parks, making it the college season pass with the most skiing in New Hampshire for $299. Valid at Cannon, Waterville Valley, Bretton Woods and Mt. Cranmore any day of the 2013-2014 season. Full time college students must prove full time status (undergraduate, 12 credits; graduate, 9 credits) with documentation from the registrar’s office i.e. letter from the registrar's/bursar's office or current college schedule PRINT out. College ID’s will not be accepted as proof. All college passes must be picked up in person by the passholder only. College passes cannot be mailed out. Season passes are non-transferable and non-refundable.
